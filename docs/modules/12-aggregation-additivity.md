# Module 12 — Aggregation, Hierarchies & Additivity ⭐

!!! abstract "Module Goal"
    The most underrated module. Knowing what can be summed (and what can't) prevents the largest class of risk reporting bugs.

!!! note "Status: Outline"
    This module's content will be expanded during the training. The outline below shows what the module will cover.

## Outline

### 12.1 The Three Categories

*To be expanded.*

Additive, semi-additive, non-additive measures.

### 12.2 Additive Measures

*To be expanded.*

Notional, sensitivities (within constraints), stress P&L. Sum across any dimension.

### 12.3 Semi-Additive Measures

*To be expanded.*

Positions, balances. Sum across books on a date — not across dates.

### 12.4 Non-Additive Measures

*To be expanded.*

VaR, ES, ratios, percentages. Must be recalculated, not summed.

### 12.5 📊 Stats Detour — Why You Can't Sum Standard Deviations

*To be expanded.*

Variance is additive (under independence); stddev is not.
